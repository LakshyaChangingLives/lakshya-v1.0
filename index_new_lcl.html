Ashraya Refurbishment Project

Ashraya School is attended by over 150 children from poor and underprivileged families in and around Hebbal, Bangalore. It is also home to many children, which include mentally challenged children as well.

Due to lack of funding, the Ashraya School building is in a dilapidated state. The roof has multiple cracks from where water falls in the class room. One of the walls is on the verge of collapsing and posses serious danger for the school going children.

Lakshya is in the process of refurbishing the Ashraya School. The estimated cost of this refurbishment is Rs 1.5 Lakh. We urge everyone to come forward and donate for this noble cause and spread the word in your circle. Every single rupee makes a difference.
