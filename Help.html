How yourDonationshelp

The priority for Lakshya is to educate the children.

The action plan includes providing an English Teacher at the orphanage at Sneha Orphanage at the highest priority. Once the children are comfortable with the language at a higher level, a computer teacher will be provided along with a lab setup to teach basics of computers.

Parallel to providing traditional education, Lakshya aims at providing vocational education to the older children at the orphanage as they walk into adulthood. The oldest children at the orphanage range from 15 to 16 years.

How Can You Donate?

At Lakshya we have a concept of commitment. Any person can commit an x amount of money for y amount of months. The amount can range from Rs. 50 to Rs. 1000 or more. The no. of months can range from 3, 6, 12 or more. Any kind of help is more than welcome!
